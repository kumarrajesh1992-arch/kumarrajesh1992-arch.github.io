About me

I am a policy technocrat with over a decade of experience across government institutions, multilateral organisations, and civil society in India—working on human development, state capacity, poverty measurement, SDG monitoring, labour market analysis, and evidence-based policymaking. I am currently a Chevening Scholar and an MPP student at the London School of Economics.

Skills: Python (pandas, sklearn), Vega-Lite, data cleaning & reproducible workflows, applied regression & diagnostic thinking, human development metrics (HDI/MPI), public finance & budgeting, policy writing and government reporting.
